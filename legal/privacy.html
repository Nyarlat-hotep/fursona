Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-29

Fursona ("the app") is a personal project by Taylor Cornelius. This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and what your rights are over it. By using the app you agree to this policy.

What we collect

Account info. If you sign in, we store your email address and an authentication identifier in our authentication provider (Supabase). If you use Google sign-in, your Google account's email and name are visible to us.

Photos and silhouettes. Photos you upload are stored privately in our storage provider (Supabase Storage). They are accessible only to you when you are signed in. Photo metadata (EXIF, GPS, camera info) is stripped before upload.

Nicknames and styling. The nicknames you type and the visual style choices for your word cloud are stored in our database against your account.

Local drafts. If you use the app without an account, a draft of your in-progress work is stored in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device.

No analytics, ads, or third-party trackers. The app does not embed analytics SDKs, advertising scripts, or tracking pixels.

How we use your data

Your data is used to operate the app: to render word clouds, save your projects, and let you sign back in. It is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing.

Where it's stored

Authentication and storage are provided by Supabase. The app uses Supabase's row-level security to ensure each user can only read and write their own data.

Your rights

Access. You can view all your saved projects from the Saves dropdown when signed in.

Deletion. You can delete individual saved projects at any time. To delete your entire account along with all photos, previews, and projects, use "Delete account" in the user menu, or email tcorneliusart@gmail.com.

Withdrawal of consent. Sign out and close the tab. No further data is collected from anonymous use beyond the local draft saved in your own browser.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes meaningfully, the "Last updated" date above will move. Continued use after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or requests: tcorneliusart@gmail.com.
